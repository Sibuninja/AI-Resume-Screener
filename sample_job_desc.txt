Job Description – Web Developer

Location: Gurugram, Haryana (Hybrid)
Experience: 0–2 years

About the Role

We are seeking a passionate Web Developer to join our growing team. The ideal candidate should have experience in building responsive web applications and working with modern front-end and back-end technologies.

Key Responsibilities

Develop and maintain responsive websites and web applications using modern frameworks.

Build scalable back-end services and integrate them with REST/GraphQL APIs.

Work with databases (SQL/NoSQL) for data-driven applications.

Collaborate with designers, product managers, and other engineers in an Agile environment.

Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.

Required Skills

Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript.

Experience with React.js (or similar frameworks like Angular/Vue).

Knowledge of Node.js, Express, and REST APIs.

Understanding of MongoDB / SQL databases.

Familiarity with Git, Docker, and cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP).

Nice-to-Have

Exposure to Django/Flask for back-end development.

Experience with GraphQL APIs.

Basic knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and containerization.
